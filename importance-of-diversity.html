Importance of Diversity

A collection of stories

Airbags and Stairs

When airbags were invented, they were considered as safety inventions that were potentially life-saving....unless you were a women or child. The all male team that produced the airbag calibrated for their body builds failing to account for women and children passengers. The results were higher mortality rates for women and children. Even to this day, mostly male test dummies are used for tests and women are more likely to suffer serious injuries in car crashes.

In 2011, a courthouse in Ohio implemented glass stairs in their new $105 million building.glass stairs. In case you didn't know, glass stairs aren't really dress and skirt friendly.

Imagine if there were more women on the team.

Facial and Image Recognition

Two years ago, an Asian engineering student was trying to renew his New Zealand passport online. Unfortunately, the algorithm used to check photos determined his eyes were closed thus rejecting his photo.

In 2015, Google's photos application labeled photos of black people as being photos of gorillas. In 2018 Google "fixed" this issue by removing the gorilla label.

Imagine if there were minority representation reflected in the tech industry.

Statistics
